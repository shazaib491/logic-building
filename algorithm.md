### Algorithm 

Algorithm ek step-by-step process hota hai jo kisi problem ko solve karne ya koi kaam complete karne ke liye instructions deta hai. Yeh instructions sequence mein arranged hote hain, jisse humari desired output milti hai. For example, agar tumhe chai banani hai, to chai banane ka algorithm kuch aisa hoga:

1. Paani garam karo.
2. Chai patti daalo.
3. Cheeni daalo.
4. Thodi der ke liye ubalne do.
5. Chaan lo aur cup mein daalo.

Yeh step-by-step instructions ek algorithm ke jaisa hi hai. Algorithm mathematically ya computer programs mein bhi use kiya jata hai, jahan hum problem ko breakdown karke solve karte hain, jaise sorting, searching ya koi aur task.

Samajhne ke liye algorithm bas ek plan hai jise follow karke hum koi kaam systematically karte hain.
